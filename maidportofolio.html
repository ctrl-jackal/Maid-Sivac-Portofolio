What I do

Digitalisation process, from dwg/raster to BIM

Development of 3D models from PDF and DWG reference drawings or from Point Cloud model. Floor Plans and Sections of buildings also included, as well as detailing and preparation of sheets for export.

Revit Asset Development - Revit Families

Creating Architectural, Structural and Mechanical parametric families in Revit. Level of details can vary based on client demands. LOD 100 - A conceptual model that derives information from other model elements LOD 200 - Schematic layout with approximate size, shape, and location LOD 300 - Modeled as design-specified size, shape, spacing, and location of equipment LOD 350 - Modeled as actual size, shape, spacing. Location and connections of equipment supplied LOD 400 - Supplementary components added to the model required for fabrication and field installation

Civil Engineering

As a bachelor of civil enginering I was working on bridge design, small houses design with calculations in FEA softwares like SAP2000 or Tower. I was also working inside Structural team during my previous work experience.

About me

Everything you need to know

I am very enthusiastic and interested in BIM technologies and softwares, which are very trending tools in AEC industry. I consider myself a really daring and openminded hard worker, who is always in the stream of constant striving for newer and better, and very willing to go outside my comfort zone, to reach new horizons beyond limits.

In the past two years, I had a chance to work in the only fully BIM-oriented company in Bosnia and Herzegovina, and I have managed to be part of every single team inside it. I have started working as a BIM Modeler in “Digital Objectification BIM” department, and by the time I was constantly seeking for new opportunities, and after a while, I have managed to get position inside “BIM Consulting and Engineering” department, where I gained a lot of experience working on the design of precast hall elements, as well as formwork and rebar plans, inside phases 4 and 5 for residential building.

In the last year, I got more workload, with simultaneously working inside “BIM Asset Development” Department where I gained much practice developing Revit Families for various clients around Europe and the USA.

One of my goals is to stay deep inside BIM, while constantly learning new things around such as Revit API Plug-ins development, or visual programming via Dynamo, which I consider as advanced stuff for much more being involved and effective in BIM and also automatize processes in a workflow.

In the last few months, I decided to go step further and started learning web development, both frontend and backend and went through HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, Node.js, jQuery and mongoDB.
